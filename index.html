About Meeting Action Agent

Why this is an Agent, not just a script:

1. Intelligent Extraction: It parses unstructured speech/text using Google Gemini LLM, mapping ambiguous timeline expressions (like "early next week" or "Thursday") to calendar dates.

2. Autonomous Automation: It acts on your behalf. By authorizing Todoist, it builds projects, configures dates, and pushes tasks directly into your task manager.

3. Follow-up Recaps: It writes and designs professional recap drafts containing task boards, saving manual data entry and email copywriting time.
